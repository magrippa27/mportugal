A safe workshop is a productive workshop. These essential safety practices will help protect you while working on projects.

## Personal Protective Equipment

Safety glasses, hearing protection, and dust masks should be used appropriately for different tasks.

## Tool Maintenance

Well-maintained tools are safer tools. Regular inspection and maintenance prevent accidents.

## Workshop Organization

A clean, organized workspace reduces the risk of accidents and makes work more efficient.
